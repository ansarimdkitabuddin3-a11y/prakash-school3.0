Social Responsibility

Community Service

We believe education extends beyond classrooms. Our students actively serve and uplift the community of Pilibanga.

Building Responsible Citizens

Prakash Model School instills social values through active community service programs, environmental drives, and awareness campaigns that benefit the people of Pilibanga and surrounding areas.

Tree Plantation Drive

Annual plantation campaign with students planting saplings across Pilibanga town and school grounds to promote green environment.

500+ trees planted

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

Regular cleanliness drives in the school neighborhood, local markets, and public spaces under the Swachh Bharat mission.

20+ events held

Blood Donation Camp

Annual blood donation camp organized with local hospitals where senior students, staff, and parents participate actively.

100+ units donated

Adult Literacy Program

Senior students volunteer to teach basic literacy skills to adults in nearby villages under the guidance of teachers.

50+ beneficiaries

Water Conservation Drive

Awareness campaigns on water conservation and rainwater harvesting conducted in schools and surrounding communities.

10+ villages reached

Annual cultural programs promoting communal harmony, national integration, and celebration of India's diverse heritage.
